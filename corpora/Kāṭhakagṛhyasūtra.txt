tṛtīye garbhamāse sīmantaṃ kārayate // (1.1)
	śuddhapakṣasya puṇyāhe parvaṇi vā yathoktam upasamādhāya jayaprabhṛtibhir hutvā paścād agner darbheṣu prāṅāsīnāyāḥ sarvān keśān sampramucya prasādhayate yaṃ sīmantam iti / (2.1)
	yaṃ sīmantaṃ kaṅkatas te cakāra yad vā kṣuraḥ parivavarja vapaṃs te / (2.2)
	strīṣu rūpam aśvinau taṃ nidhattaṃ pauṃsyenemaṃ saṃsṛjataṃ vīryeṇa / (2.3)
	indrāṇī cakre kaṅkataṃ sa sīmantaṃ visarpatu / (2.4)
	punaḥ patibhyo jāyāṃ dā agne prajayā saha / (2.5)
	punaḥ patnīm agnir adād āyuṣā saha varcasā / (2.6)
	dīrghāyur asyā yaḥ patir jīvāti śaradaḥ śatam iti // (2.7)
	triḥśyetayā śalalyā śamīśākhayā sapalāśayā vā sīmantaṃ vicinoti yās te rāka iti // (3.1)
	athāsyāḥ pṛthak keśapakṣau saṃnahyati nīlalohitena sūtreṇa nīlalohitaṃ bhavati kṛtyāsaktir vyajyate / (4.1)
	edhante asyā jñātayaḥ patir bandheṣu badhyatām iti // (4.2)
	yo gurus tam arhayet // (5.1)
	etad eva kumārīṇāṃ saṭoddharaṇam atraivānulepanam // (6.1)
