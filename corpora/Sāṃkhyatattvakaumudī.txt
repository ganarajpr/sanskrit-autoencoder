	etāvatā pratikūlavedanīyatvaṃ jihāsāhetur uktam / (1.20)
	yadyapi na saṃnirudhyate duḥkhaṃ tathāpi tadabhibhavaḥ śakyaṃ kartum ityupariṣṭānnivedayiṣyate / (1.21)
	tathā copapannaṃ tadapaghātake hetāviti / (1.22)
	tasya duḥkhatrayasyāpaghātakas tadapaghātakaḥ / (1.23)

	apaghātakaś ca hetuḥ śāstravyutpādyo nānya ityāśayaḥ / (1.25)

	ayam arthaḥ / (1.27)
	astu duḥkhatrayaṃ jihāsatu ca taccetano bhavatu ca tacchakyahānaṃ sahatāṃ ca śāstragamya upāyastad ucchettum / (1.28)
	tathāpyatra prekṣāvatāṃ jijñāsāyuktā / (1.29)
	dṛṣṭasyaivopāyasya taducchedakasya sukarasya vidyamānatvāttattvajñānasya tv anekajanmābhyāsaparamparāyāsasādhyatayātiduṣkaratvāt / (1.30)
	tathā ca laukikānām ābhāṇakaḥ / (1.31)
	arke cenmadhu vindeta kim arthaṃ parvataṃ vrajet / (1.32)
