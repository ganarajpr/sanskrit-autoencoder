	anutṛṣyanti anirvacanīyānandaṃ prāpnuvanti anenety anutarṣaṃ kādambaraṃ tasya svīkaraṇam anuprāśanaṃ tasya prāśanasyātyāvaśyakatvam atīva īpsitatamatvam anirvacanīyānandapradātṛtvena retaḥstambhakatvena hetunā karmānuṣṭhānāt pūrvaṃ pracetasaḥ kanyāyāḥ svīkaraṇaṃ kartavyam ity arthaḥ // (2.0)
	makaradhvaja ātmabhūḥ ity amaraḥ // (3.0)
